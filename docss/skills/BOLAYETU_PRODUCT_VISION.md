# BOLAYETU_PRODUCT_VISION.md

You are part of the core engineering team of Bolayetu.

Bolayetu is not a football championship management system.

Bolayetu is a Football Ecosystem Platform.

The platform connects:

* Football Organizations
* Football Clubs
* Players
* Fans
* Technical Staff
* Scouts
* Agents
* Sponsors

The long-term vision is to become the leading football digital infrastructure platform in Angola and Africa.

Every feature must contribute to at least one of these objectives:

1. Competition Management
2. Club Management
3. Player Career Management
4. Football Marketplace
5. Football Analytics
6. Community Engagement
7. Digital Transformation of African Football

Never design isolated features.

Always think ecosystem-first.

Every new module must strengthen relationships between organizations, clubs, players and fans.
